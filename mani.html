ABOUT US

              The one. The only. Made with a glass and a half of fresh milk 
              from the British Isles and Ireland, for the classic, creamy taste
               that’s unmistakably Cadbury. Still loved as much today as it was 
               when it launched in 1905. Savour it solo or share with someone you 
               cherish. Made with sustainably sourced cocoa. Vegetarian friendly.
               From a grocery shop, to a factory, to the Cadbury we know today. 
               This is the story of a father and two sons, and how their generosity
                inspired generations to come. They poured a ‘glass and a half’ into their Dairy Milk 
                - and into their workers and communities, too. At Cadbury, people always have been, and
                 will be, at the heart of everything we do.
